Advertise with us...

If you're interested in advertising an upcoming concert, gig, or just generally advertising yourself as a musician, you can send us promotional material and we can give you exposure on our front page. Advertising is limited to MMSS members and costs $5 for continuous advertising for all your upcoming musical events.

Instructions

Send an email to music@monashclubs.org with the following details:
Subject: Web Promotion
Name of group/band/artist
Name of event/concert/gig (if relevant)
A very short blurb (50 words max) about the event (if relevant)
A promotional image of either the event or the artist/s

Image Requirements

File type: JPEG
Resolution: 400px x 400px
Low Resolution - 'Save for Web' via Photoshop
If Photoshop is not available, please submit a high resolution of any format (400px x 400px) and the file will be converted to low resolntion.

By applying to advertise with us on our website, you are agreeing to our Terms and Conditions.
